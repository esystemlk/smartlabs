## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette:
- **Primary**: Pastel Blue (HSL 217, 91%, 60%)
- **Accent-1**: Pastel Pink (HSL 347, 89%, 68%)
- **Accent-2**: Pastel Green (HSL 159, 70%, 55%)
- **Accent-3**: Pastel Purple (HSL 260, 90%, 75%)
- **Accent-4**: Pastel Orange (HSL 38, 92%, 60%)

### Typography:
- **Display Font**: Poppins (headings)
- **Body Font**: Inter (paragraphs)
- **Sizes**: Responsive from 2xl to 7xl

### Effects:
- **Glass Morphism**: Backdrop blur with transparency
- **Gradients**: Multi-color gradients throughout
- **Animations**: Floating, pulsing, rotating, scaling
- **Shadows**: Layered shadows for depth

## 📱 Responsive Design

### Breakpoints:
- **Mobile**: < 640px (1 column, compact spacing)
- **Tablet**: 640px - 1024px (2 columns)
- **Desktop**: > 1024px (3-4 columns, floating cards)

### Mobile Optimizations:
- Full-width buttons
- Stacked layouts
- Reduced padding
- Smaller text sizes
- Hidden floating cards (shown as static on mobile)
